Briefing:

Operation Archery: The commando raid in the Bergen area of Norway. The Unterseebootwaffe of the 11th Flotilla is harassing Allied shipping in the North Sea. As Operation Arrow descends on the Bergen area of Norway the Axis must protect the base from Allied attempts at sabotage. As part of Operation Archery, the Allies must destroy the Transmitter at the submarine base located west of the town. The Axis forces must defend the Transmitter at all costs until reinforcements arrive.
