Region:Central Europe (02) / UPPER

Briefing:

Allied intelligence have discovered that the Axis forces are temporarily storing a large shipment of gold at a supply depot near the German border. The Allies have to drive a truck into the supply depot to steal the crate of gold using a crane. The Axis have to defend the Depot and protect the Gold.
