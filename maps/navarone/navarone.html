Briefing:

The Axis are planning to execute over 2000 English soldiers on the Greek island of Kheros. The Allied forces plan a rescue by sea, but to do so, must sail past the island of Navarone. On Navarone the Axis have two huge cannons inside a cliffwall with a shooting range of 200 kilometers. The Allied mission is to destroy the cannons from inside the fortress in the cliffside. If they fail, many lives will be lost.
