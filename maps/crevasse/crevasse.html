Briefing:

The Underground have discovered the location of an Axis facility built inconspicuously into an natural crevasse. Operatives report that there is valuable information kept somewhere in the base. The Allies must obtain this information and transmit it to Allied Command. The Axis must prevent this, and defend the base.
