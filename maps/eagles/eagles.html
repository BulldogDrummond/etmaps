Briefing:

The Allies have discovered that a list of Axis agents is being kept at Schloss Adler, the Castle of Eagles. In order to prevent them from obtaining it, the Axis must stop the Allied troops from reaching the castle and flying the document out of the country from nearby Oberhausen airfield.
