Region:Northern Europe (03) / UPPER
Conditions:DAY / CLEAR-SNOW
Defending:Axis
Time Limit:30 minutes

Briefing:

Civilians manipulated by the Volksempfanger's propaganda program are resisting the Allied forces. To break the resistance, the Allies have to take gain control of the Radio Transmitter, which is amplifying the master signal from Berlin for local receivers. They must manipulate the transmission-frequency by installing a Radio Modification Kit on the Transmitter. The Allies also have to rotate the Antenna toward the pirate sender to get the signal through.
